About BUGG

Run by Community Decentralize Platform

BUGG Finance (BUGG) is a community-focused, decentralized cryptocurrency with instant rewards for holders.

Buy and Hold BUGG and you will automatically get more in your wallet. No need to farm, stake or claim.

Each trade also automatically adds liquidity on Pancakeswap leading to an ever-increasing price floor of BUGG. This means that even if every single BUGG holder sells, the price will not go to zero.

Process

Buying process for BUGG Finance Token

Step 1

Create a Trust Wallet
  • BUGG Finance is available on the Binance Smart Chain. Trust Wallet is the market leader in BEP20 (Binance) wallets. Get Trust Wallet's app for iPhone or Android from App Store or Play Store.

Step 2

Send BNB to Trust Wallet
  • Transfer BNB to your Trust wallet address from another wallet (e.g. Coinbase or Binance).

Step 3

Visit PancakeSwap
  • You can currently buy BUGG on Pancakeswap, For that click on Dapp and open Pancakeswap from there.

Step 4

Swap BNB for BUGG
  • Enter the amount of BNB you would like to swap for BUGG. Click Connect Wallet then Swap.
